Minutes of our Meetings

Wednesday, September 23, 1998, 3:30 p.m.
 
Nominations were conducted and the following were chosen to serve as officers:
              President:                Beth Burton
              Vice President:           Stephanie Elliot
              Secretary:                Kim Kramer      
              Treasurer:                Gena Statema
We discussed the dues that needed to be paid, $5 for State and $9 for National. It was voted that the club would pay for the dues. We talked about going to the Fall Leadership Conference in Des Moines on October 21 and 22. The following have decided to attend the conference: Beth, Kim, Mike, Chris H., Lecia, and Gena. We discussed ideas for fundraising and activities we could do as a group. Some of the fundraising ideas were selling bottled water in the Book Store and having a Bake Sale. Some group activities were having a winter clothes drive and going to the nursing home. We talked about doing fun activities as a club by going skiing, tubing, and having a Christmas party. Treats were brought by Stephanie Elliot.
The next meeting will be on Wednesday, October 14,1998 at 3:30 p.m. Sheryl decided that she would furnish the group with treats.

Wednesday, October 14, 1998, 3:30 p.m.
 
Beth Burton began at 3:30 p.m. The minutes of the previous meeting were read. No Treasurer's report was given.
New Business:
  1. We filled out magazine subscription form for the Business Professionals of America magazine.
  2. We received Business Professionals of America's Iowa Association Newsletter regarding the 1998 National Competitive Event Winners.
  3. We discussed giving blood for the Bloodmobile on Friday, October 16, 1998. Some of us were going to work while others were going to give blood.
  4. We received papers about the Torch Awards. We discussed ideas that we could do to get some of these points for a Torch award.
  5. We discussed our upcoming trip to the Fall Leadership Conference in Des Moines.
  6. We discussed carving pumpkins for the carving contest. We are going to carve pumpkins on Wednesday, October 28. Lecia is going to bring treats.
  7. We discussed donating two movie tickets for the Alumni Membership Drive. Steph made a motion to donate the tickets and Sheryl seconded her motion.
 
Beth told us that if there are ever any announcements that happen to come up before our next meeting, she will put them in the daily announcement sheet that the college puts out.
Another fundraising idea was brought up. It was having us sell pictures of famous people that are in frames to students on campus.
The next meeting will be on Wednesday, November 11, 1998 at 3:30 p.m. Kim decided that she would furnish the group with treats.

Wednesday, November 11, 1998, 3:30 p.m.
 
Beth Burton began the meeting at 3:30 p.m. The minutes of the previous meeting were read. No Treasurer's report was given.
Business:
  1. We discussed having a Winter Clothing Drive and Gena volunteered to make a flier to hang around the buildings.
  2. We received our Business Professionals of America cards.
  3. We discussed having a Fund-raiser. We decided to sell raffle tickets for $1 a piece to win 1 of 2 gift certificates for $25 to any place of the winner's choice.
  4. We won third place at the pumpkin carving contest.
  5. We discussed adopting a family for Christmas.
 
The next meeting will be on Wednesday, December 2, 1998 at 3:30 p.m. We decided to have a Christmas party at the next meeting, so we are all bringing treats to share. Everyone is to bring there raffle tickets back on December 2,1998.

Wednesday, December 2, 1998, 3:30 p.m.
 
Beth Burton began the meeting at 3:30 p.m. The minutes of the previous meeting were read. No Treasurer's report was given.
Business:
  1. We discussed how our Winter Clothing Drive was going.
  2. We collected our money and tickets from our raffle.
  3. We discussed membership and $450.00 for registration was outstanding. Hotel costs of $200.00 will be taken from the balance, leaving us with $416.06.
    4. A motion for a bake sale was made by Steph and seconded by Heidi. Plans for the bake sale were discussed and the sale is to be next Thursday January 28th.
    The next meeting will be Feb. 26, 1999.
