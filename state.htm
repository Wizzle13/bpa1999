IOWA LAKES COMMUNITY COLLEGE STUDENTS PLACE AT NATIONAL CONFERENCE AND WIN PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION

BUSINESS STUDENTS ADVANCE TO NATIONALS

    Eighteen business professional students from Iowa Lakes Community College earned top honors at the state competition, held March 3 - 5, in Cedar Rapids. In addition, LaRae Banwart from West Bend, Cassie Sniezek from Garner and Carolann Fullerton from Peterson will be representing the state of Iowa as a state officer and voting delegate at the national conference.

    The Iowa Lakes winners competed with nearly 200 other students from 13 colleges. In addition to the individual achievements, the Spencer Chapter received the Outstanding Service Award for Special Olympics for the State of Iowa. Professors accompanying the students to the state competition included: Lecia Berven, Accounting Specialist Instructor; Deb Jones, Office Technology Professor; and, Becky Simons, Assistant Professor of Business.
